Howdy Howdy Hello!

This is the personal, professional, emotional, metaphysical, antinomical website of Sam Macdonald.
This site is still heavily under construction, so what works works and what doesn't doesn't!

I am a second year graduate teaching assistant at the University of Nebraska -- Lincoln working towards a Ph.D. in mathematics. I graduated from Willamette Univerity in the Spring of 2020 with a BA in Mathematics and a minor in politics.

As of now I am interested in figuring out what my interests are, though they most likely lie in Math Education, Algebra, or Analysis. Or goat herding. I'm not particularly picky at the moment

When not pouring my life into either teaching or learning math, you can find me climbing, reading by the lake, meticulously catelogueing all the music I listen to, or neglecting my cats.

This website is mostly professional, but its also a place where I can keep track of all my interests and projects that sometimes go for years without me thinking about them. Feel free to poke around the personal section if you'd like, and don't forget to like and subscribe.
